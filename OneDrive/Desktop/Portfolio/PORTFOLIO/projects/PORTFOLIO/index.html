About Me

Get to know a little thing about me

Hello! I’m Ericka Gabutin, a passionate BS Information Technology student at Cavite State University – Silang Campus. I’m dedicated to building responsive and user‑centered websites, exploring UI/UX designs, and writing clear and helpful technical content. I enjoy creating solutions that not only look good but are intuitive and usable.

I believe in continuous learning and hands‑on practice to grow as a developer, designer, and writer.

My goal is to grow into a Front‑End Web Developer, UI/UX Designer, or Technical Writer who builds meaningful digital experiences that solve real user needs. Feel free to check out my projects or contact me if you’d like to connect!
